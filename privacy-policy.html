Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tap Ball ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Tap Ball does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

All game data (player names, scores, coin balance, purchased items) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements (banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads). AdMob may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and app information
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. For more information, see: Google Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Tap Ball is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertisements shown are provided by Google AdMob and are subject to Google's own policies regarding ad content.

4. Data Storage

All user preferences and game progress are stored locally on your device using shared preferences. No data is transmitted to external servers by us. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

5. Permissions

The App requires internet access solely for loading and displaying advertisements via Google AdMob. No other special permissions are required.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
